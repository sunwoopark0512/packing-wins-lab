# Voice & Style Guide

## 1. Hook (0-2s)
- Must be visual or controversial.
- Example: "Stop packing air." / "Your suitcase is lying to you."
- Max 1 sentence.

## 2. Body (Shorts)
- 3-5 sentences max.
- Structure: Problem (Pain) -> Fix (Product/Method) -> Proof (Visual).
- **Reddit Tone**: Detailed, community-focused, "I found this helpful", NO "Buy this now".

## 3. CTA (Call to Action)
- Short and direct.
- "Link is in bio." (KO: "링크는 프로필에.")
- "List in comments." (KO: "리스트는 댓글에.")

## 4. Language Mix
- **KO**: Friendly, respectful but concise (해요체).
- **EN**: Casual, direct, concise.
