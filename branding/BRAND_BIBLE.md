# Brand Bible: Packing Wins
**One Line Promise**: "We help travelers pack lighter, smarter, and faster."
**Positioning**: Practical, No-nonsense, Visual-first.

## 1. Forbidden Terms (Banned)
- "Guaranteed" (Don't promise results we can't control)
- "Magic pill" / "Secret hack" (Too clickbaity)
- "Get rich" / "Make money" (Irrelevant)
- "Best in the world" (Subjective, use "Our favorite" or "Top rated")

## 2. CTA Philosophy
- **One Action Only**: Never ask for Like + Comment + Share + Link. Pick ONE.
- **LinkHub Focus**: "Grab yours in bio" / "Link in comments" (Drive traffic to Hub).

## 3. Tone
- **Helpful**: Focus on solving the messy suitcase problem.
- **Clean**: Visuals should be satisfying and organized.
